# Blinded Review Rubric (Cardio Universe)

Each reviewer scores every opportunity on a 1-5 scale.

Criteria:
- Clinical relevance in cardiology.
- Methodological validity of grouping/outcome mapping.
- Novelty versus already-saturated evidence syntheses.
- Actionability (can this be turned into a review quickly).
- Transparency/provenance confidence.

Decision guidance:
- `switch_now`: median score >=4 with no red-flag criterion <=2.
- `switch_with_conditions`: median >=3 with remediable issues.
- `not_yet`: median <3 or major trust/validity concerns.

Required comments:
- One strength.
- One risk.
- One required improvement.

Blocking red flags:
- Untraceable score derivation.
- Incorrect outcome harmonization.
- Unsafe or misleading confidence labeling.
