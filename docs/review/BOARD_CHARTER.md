# 12-Person Shura Methodology Board Charter

## Purpose
Provide blinded, high-rigor external judgment on whether the Cardio Universe platform is superior enough to replace current workflow.

## Membership (12)
- 4 systematic review / meta-analysis methodologists
- 3 cardiologists (different subspecialties)
- 2 biostatisticians
- 1 information specialist / librarian
- 1 guideline-methods expert
- 1 patient/public voice

## Core Rules
- No member reviews their own contributed opportunities.
- Decision judgments are blinded to source implementation details.
- Disagreements must include written rationale and evidence references.
- Every cycle logs both majority and dissent reasoning.

## Decision Scale
- `switch_now`
- `switch_with_conditions`
- `not_yet`

## Adoption Gate
- Operational target: `>=11/12` reviewers with `switch_now` in two consecutive cycles.

## Cadence
- Biweekly cycle.
- Mid-cycle calibration check (method only, not rankings).
- End-cycle decision publication and remediation list.
